In May 2023 we delivered a talk for the Henry Royce Institute Stand Out Skills webinar series. Websites are a great way to promote yourself, your research, or other projects you participate in. Adapting one of the many freely available website templates allows you to build a personal brand and communicate effectively. In this talk, Lucy walked us through how to set up a free, static (non-interactive) website using Github Pages and highlighted some effective examples from materials science research.
